Select your proxy type and enter your proxy value. The PayNow proxy value refers to the unique indicator of the recipient which  may be used to receive payments via PayNow.

- PayNow proxy should be within 8 to 17 characters.

- Acceptable PayNow proxy values are as follows:

- Individuals: NRIC/FIN Number (e.g. S1234567X)

- Corporates: UEN (e.g. 201234567A) or UEN + 3-character suffix (e.g. 201234567A B12 , 201234567A 321 ), where a 3-character alphanumeric suffix can be added to your company's UEN to create multiple PayNow proxies. This is based off what proxy is registered with your bank and the respective bank account.
